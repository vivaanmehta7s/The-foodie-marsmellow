Student-Initiated Creative Project

About the Project

The Foodie Marshmallow is a student-initiated project developed by students of The Shri Ram School – Aravali, Gurugram, India. What began as a simple idea gradually evolved into a creative and engaging concept that reflects innovation, curiosity, and collaborative learning.

The platform used for this company website was designed and developed by one of the co-founders, along with an associated game created by the student Chief Technology Officer (CTO). The project highlights the students’ strong interest in technology, creativity, and problem-solving through digital design.

The Foodie Marshmallow represents our commitment to learning through creation, and we hope it is received as an example of student effort, imagination, and technical growth.
